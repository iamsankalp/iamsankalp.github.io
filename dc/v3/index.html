Hrush Bhatt
Co-founder
Hrush graduated from Lawrence University, Appleton, Wisconsin, with Bachelor of Arts degrees in Economics and History. Returning to India in 1998, he worked with Plexus Technologies for a year, serving as lead designer and founding their ecommerce strategy practice. While at Plexus, Hrush also served as lead consultant for the launch of Indiatimes.com. In 1999, he co-founded Paper Plane, a boutique consulting firm focussed on business strategy and design in the online space.
Sunit Singh
Co-founder
As the Design Head, Sunit is responsible for the user experience of products across Ola. He's been instrumental in setting the tone for Ola Mobile & led the recent redesign. Sunit graduated from J. Varun Gupta
Co-founder
